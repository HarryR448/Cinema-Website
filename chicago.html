Synopsis

The Chicago musical is the kiss-and-tell story of chorus girl Roxie who kills her lover; Velma Kelly, the glamorous double-murderer and Billy Flynn, the slick and manipulative lawyer keeping them from death row while promising to make them stars, has all the topicality of our celebrity-seeking times.

Written by John Kander and Fred Ebb, Chicago has choreography by the legendary Bob Fosse and won the Olivier Award for Outstanding Musical Production. The Chicago songs include All That Jazz, Cell Block Tango and Razzle Dazzle.
